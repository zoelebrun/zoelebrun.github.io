TECHNICAL INFORMATION

-- suite corporis (heart, lungs, brain) --

Submitted December 2023, for CLUSTER FESTIVAL application


Technical needs are fairly straightforward for this project. The table at the bottom describes which equipment I have already and which items I would need provided.

For the sound performance, I will need 4 PA speakers to adapt soundscape channels to space, XLR cables, 1/4” cables, 1 audio interface and related cabling, a laptop, instruments, 1 music stand, and 2 microphones to loop two live elements at once.

For the accompanying video, I will require 1 projector and 1 screen (or a clean white wall).
