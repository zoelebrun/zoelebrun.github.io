untitled (installation 1)

multichannel video installation with personal and found objects


Documentation from temporary installation at the University of Manitoba


Reflections on the processes of memory, identity formation, and the way that universal experiences become personally coded.

Who do you become when the memories fragment and fade? How do we embed ourselves into the world around us?
