untitled (a ball), 2021


Zoë LeBrun
untitled (a ball)
2021
gloss gel image transfer, graphite, charcoal, marker, and oil pastel on canvas



untitled (a ball) is a multimedia artwork that uses and reflects on the process of automatism. Through this process, I merged personal written thoughts with drawing and image transfers to create an abstract work which reveals itself over time. Ultimately this piece is centered around the concept of play and experimentation as a vital part of rest, which our current culture encourages us, and sometimes forces us, to dismiss in favour of 9-5 routine.

This work was pivotal in my exploration of utilizing gel image transfers within painting, expanding my notions of what this genre can represent and share with us.
