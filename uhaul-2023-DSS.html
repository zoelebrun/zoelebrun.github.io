The 'Daylight Savings Society: ARThaul Exhibition' was the first public exhibition of recent artwork from Winnipeg-based artist collective the Daylight Savings Society.

In response to a lack of accessible exhibition spaces, the Daylight Savings Society decided to rent a 20 ft UHAUL truck and fill it with art, acting as a guerrilla exhibition to join Winnipeg's Nuit Blanche festivities on September 23, 2023. The artworks presented included paintings, videos, sound art, collages, drawings, and a zine that viewers could take home with them.

The exhibition featured DSS members Scotland Cook, Carlos Cruz, Zoë LeBrun, and Morgan Traa.
